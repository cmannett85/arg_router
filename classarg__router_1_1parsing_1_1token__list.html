Detailed Description

List of tokens.

This is similar in implementation to a boost::container::devector. It consists of the two views of the command line token array: pending and processed. Initially all tokens are pending and then as they processed by the nodes, they mark the tokens as processed, which moves them to the back of the processed view.

Of course we don't really keep two separate lists, there's only one, and marking them as processed simply moves the pointer that separates the two views of the container.
